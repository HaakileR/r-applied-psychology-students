# Zenodo Description

Open educational and research training materials for students of applied psychology, focused on data analysis in psychological research using R.

The repository includes:

- synthetic psychology-style dataset;
- annotated R scripts;
- data cleaning examples;
- scale scoring and reliability analysis;
- descriptive statistics;
- visualization with ggplot2;
- t-test, ANOVA, correlation, and regression examples;
- exercises for students;
- solution script;
- teaching notes;
- reporting templates.

The materials are designed for introductory teaching, workshops, and self-study in applied psychology, psychology, social sciences, research methods, and statistics.

The dataset is fully synthetic and does not contain real participant data.
